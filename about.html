Who We Are

The **IT Club of Lenana School** is a dynamic, student-led organization dedicated to fostering a passion for **technology, coding, cybersecurity, and innovation**. Established with the goal of empowering young minds, the club has grown into a hub of creativity, problem-solving, and **real-world tech exploration**.

Our Mission

To equip students with **cutting-edge IT skills**, promote **digital literacy**, and encourage **innovation** in solving real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To be a **leading technology club** that nurtures young innovators, preparing them for future careers in **software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and entrepreneurship**.
